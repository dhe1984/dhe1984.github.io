Getting Here

Travel & Stay

Everything you need to know to find us — and rest well after the celebrations.

🏛️

The Signet Library

Parliament Square
Edinburgh, EH1 1RF

Located on the Royal Mile, just beside St Giles' Cathedral in the heart of the Old Town.

🚆

By Train

Edinburgh Waverley station is a short 5-minute walk from the Signet Library. Direct services from Glasgow, London, and most major UK cities.

✈️

By Air

Edinburgh Airport is approximately 25 minutes by tram (Princes Street stop) or taxi. The tram runs directly into the city centre.

🚕

Taxis & Rideshare

Uber, Bolt and Edinburgh's famous black cabs all serve the city centre. We recommend pre-booking for the end of the evening.

🏨

Accommodation

We recommend staying in the Old Town for convenience. The Radisson Collection, The Scotsman Hotel and Hotel du Vin are all within easy walking distance.

Please book early — Edinburgh in October fills up quickly!

🎩

The Ceremony

Our ceremony takes place at Linlithgow at 1:15pm. Transport will be provided. Please look out for further details closer to the date.
